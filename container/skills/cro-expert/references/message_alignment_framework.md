# Three-Way Message Alignment Framework (Ads → LP → SEO)

This framework provides a structured protocol for analyzing the alignment between ad copy, landing page messaging, and organic search intent. Misalignment here is a primary cause of high bounce rates and low conversion rates.

## Analysis Protocol

This analysis should be performed after collecting data from Google Ads, the landing page itself, and Ubersuggest.

### Step 1: Deconstruct the Ad

- **Ad Headline**: What is the primary promise made in the ad?
- **Ad Description**: What are the key benefits or features mentioned?
- **Ad Keywords**: (If available) Which keywords triggered the ad?

### Step 2: Deconstruct the Landing Page (Above the Fold)

- **LP Headline (H1)**: What is the main headline of the landing page?
- **LP Subheadline**: What is the supporting message?
- **LP Hero Visual**: What does the main image or video communicate?

### Step 3: Deconstruct the Search Intent (Ubersuggest)

- **Top Organic Keywords**: What are the top 3-5 keywords the landing page ranks for organically?
- **Search Intent**: Are these keywords informational ("how to..."), transactional ("buy..."), or navigational ("brand name")?
- **High-Volume Related Keywords**: What are the highest-volume related keywords that the page *doesn't* rank for?

## Step 5: Generate Recommendations

Based on the mismatches identified in the Alignment Matrix, generate specific, actionable recommendations.

### Scenario 1: Ad ↔ LP Mismatch

- **Problem**: The ad promises "50% Off Sale" but the landing page headline is "The Ultimate B2B Tool".
- **Recommendation Type**: **Fix the Landing Page** (usually easier than changing ad copy).
- **Specific Action**: Change the LP headline to "Get 50% Off The Ultimate B2B Tool". Add a banner with the sale details.

### Scenario 2: LP ↔ Search Intent Mismatch

- **Problem**: The landing page headline is "Start Your Free Trial" but the top organic keyword is "B2B tool pricing".
- **Recommendation Type**: **Test a New Landing Page Headline**.
- **Specific Action**: A/B test the current headline against "Transparent Pricing for The Ultimate B2B Tool". Change the primary CTA to "See Pricing".

### Scenario 3: Ad ↔ Search Intent Mismatch

- **Problem**: The ad focuses on "Easy Collaboration" but top organic keywords are all about "data security".
- **Recommendation Type**: **Test New Ad Copy**.
- **Specific Action**: Create a new ad group with headlines and descriptions focused on "data security", "secure platform", etc. Direct this ad group to the same landing page, but consider adding a "Security" section to the page.

### Scenario 4: All Three Are Misaligned (Worst Case)

- **Problem**: Ad says "Free Trial", LP says "Request a Demo", and search intent is for "Pricing".
- **Recommendation Type**: **Full Funnel Re-alignment**.
- **Specific Action**: This is a strategic issue. The recommendation is to first define the primary offer (trial, demo, or direct purchase) and then align the ad copy, landing page, and keyword targeting strategy around that single offer.

---

## Output & Delivery

- **Include the Alignment Matrix** in the final audit report.
- **Create a dedicated "Message Alignment Analysis" section** in the report.
- **Prioritize recommendations**: A full-funnel re-alignment is a major strategic fix, while a simple headline change is a quick win or A/B test.
- **Suggest tests for both Ads and Landing Page**: Sometimes the ad is wrong, sometimes the page is wrong. Provide recommendations for both.
