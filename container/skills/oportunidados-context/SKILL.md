### Key Financial Metrics (Critical for Paid Ads Analysis)

- **Average Purchase Value (First Transaction)**: R$137.61
- **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)**: R$484.91
- **Average Customer Lifespan**: 3 months
- **Monthly Churn Rate**: 28.4%
- **Target Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: R$200.00
- **Target CAC:LTV Ratio**: 1:2.42 (healthy SaaS benchmark)

### Profitability Thresholds for Paid Ads

Given the LTV of R$484.91 and target CAC of R$200:

- **Maximum Acceptable CPA**: R$200.00 (target CAC)
- **Break-even CPA**: ~R$484.91 (at LTV, before considering gross margin)
- **Target ROAS (First Purchase)**: R$137.61 / R$200 = **0.69x minimum** (based on first transaction value)
- **Target ROAS (LTV-based)**: R$484.91 / R$200 = **2.42x ideal** (based on full customer lifetime value)

**Important**: Because Oportunidados is a SaaS business with recurring revenue, a **first-purchase ROAS as low as 0.69x can be profitable** if the customer stays for the average 3-month lifespan. Campaigns should be evaluated on **CAC vs. LTV**, not just first-purchase ROAS.
